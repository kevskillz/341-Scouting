2022-2023 Programming Team Reigns Supreme

The Tech

This is the website for Team 341's scouting app. It currently takes input from our SQL databases and uses information from the Scanner App to create graphs that help visualize different constructs in games such as points, efficiency and many others.

The Purpose

We decided to make this website this year to help aid new members on the team of the beauty of scouting. In creating this site, the data from all matches will help to serve us in future matches and give us insight on what we and other teams needs to work on.

It also makes scouting easier, safer, and better. Also saves paper and anger!
